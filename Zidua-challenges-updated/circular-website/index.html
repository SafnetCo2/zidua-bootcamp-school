We bring your ideas
to life

We are a one-stop-shop for Circular Economy Excellence in Africa, inspiring and enabling the transition to a sustainable world.

Our mission is to be an active part of fast-tracking the development towards
a more circular and sustainable society, by creating a collaborative platform
where stakeholders can grow, meet, influence and be seen.

What we offer

We create a platform where innovators, entrepreneurs, corporates, academia and policy makers can co-create circular solutions in an
environment of trust and experimentation to address climate change, bio diversity loss and pollution

Accelerator Program

Our accelerator program is designed to walk with you from idea generation to scale up, connecting you with the right mentors, partners, opportunities and investors to see your idea come to life.

Advisory Services

We are the partner of choice in catalysing all your circularity ideas from conception to reality. Our portfolio of industry experts will help you understand and apply circular economy principles and sustainability.

Co-working Space & Events

We create a conducive environment at our location for office space, conferences, networking events and corporate meetings. You can also make use of our spacious garden for your events.

Corporate Training

We provide a sustainability tool kit to corporate entities to help you transition towards the circular economy and reduce your carbon footprint.
